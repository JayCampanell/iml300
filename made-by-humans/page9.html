Those images were types of images that data labellers see, especially when working on content moderation


For a company to be able to train their LLMs or other models to not give harmful content the model must learn what harmful content is.

In order to learn, it needs data, terabytes worth of data.

Typically to just learn one label the model needs over 1000 examples.

When labelling they are often given no guidance, spending 20 hours in a room with no light labeling images containing sucicide, murder, necrophilia, child sexual abuse, terrorism.
