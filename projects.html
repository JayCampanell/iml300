Project 2: HyperNarrative

Made by Humans: The Workers Behind AI

I wanted to highlight the exploitation of data annotators in the global south and how they are at the short end of the stick when it omes to the AI boom. AI is hyped up to be this magic thing that can solve all but in reality it is a product of labor and people, not only the researchers being lauded and traded but also everyday workers who have to sift through content that no one should have to, all for the promise of safety and moderation
